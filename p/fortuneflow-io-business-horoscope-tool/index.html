FortuneFlow.io – Business Horoscope Generator

An ongoing project exploring how astrological symbolism might be reinterpreted for professional contexts. FortuneFlow generates business-themed horoscopes based on a user’s role, birth details, and (optionally) current location. The result is a personalised reading that blends astronomical data with AI-generated narrative.

How It Works

The backend calculates planetary positions using Swiss Ephemeris and feeds that into a custom prompt for a large language model. On the front-end, there’s location autocomplete (via Geoapify), geolocation support, and form persistence to create a smooth and intuitive user experience.

What I’m Exploring

This project sits at the intersection of narrative design, technical implementation, and venture building. I’m exploring how interpretive tools — traditionally associated with personal introspection — can be adapted to support reflection and strategic thinking in a business setting.

From a commercial perspective, I’m testing whether there’s a viable market for tools that offer personalised insight without falling into generic self-help language. The goal isn’t to replicate astrology wholesale, but to reframe its symbolic richness in a way that resonates with professionals — particularly founders and decision-makers who value perspective, pattern recognition, and psychological framing.

Where It’s At

The prototype is live and evolving. I’m iterating on prompt design, observing user engagement, and considering how this could grow into a broader offering — whether as a standalone product or as a component within a larger toolkit for business reflection and planning.

Demo DJ Mix for Open Call

A DJ collective in Poland called Miazmat did an open call and I put this mix together as my submisssion. I tried new things with imperfect but pleasing results.

The Mix



My Approach (and deviations)

Normally I'm mixing, breaks, UKG and Drum 'n' Bass but I was inspired by the energy in the "DJ snow" video to mix some bass house.

I was tipped off the collective gets bored hearing the same style transitions so I deviated from my norm of playing it safe a little. Instead, I tried a tighter mixing style with greater variety of transitions, including some rooted in FX.

The Outcome

Ultimately, Miazmat had ~135 submissions for their open call and only 4 slots on the line-up. They said they would dip into the submissions again when organising future events so fingers crossed I'll get to play with them one day.

Reflections

In retrospect it was probably a bit of gamble to try too many new things for a demo mix hence a result that is a little rough around the edges. For instance, I realised upon playback of the recording that, at some point, I'd accidentally disabled the "MASTER TEMPO"/"pitch lock" feature on my DJ system after which the pitch of some tracks shifted during beatmatching. Oops.

Conclusion

Despite the odd mistake and indeterminate outcome of the submission, I wanted to push myself to things outside of my comfort zone and I'm glad to say, in that respect, I succeeded.

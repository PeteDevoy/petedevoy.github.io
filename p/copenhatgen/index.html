Copenhatgen

AI country song.

Imagine going to a country music festival in Copenhagen, thematically dressed in your fancy rancher hat only to have wild wind of the Øresund blow it right off of your head.

It is precisely what happened to my friend and, when she told me, I couldn't help but think that the story itself sounded like the subject of a country song.

Naturally, ChatGPT wrote the lyrics for me and I used Suno.com to generate the song.
